# Day 4: Modern ML & The Bigger Picture

Welcome to the final day! Day 4 takes us to the cutting edge of Artificial Intelligence (الذكاء الاصطناعي). We'll explore Deep Learning, how Large Language Models work, and the ethical responsibility of building AI systems.

## What we are covering today:
* **Neural Networks (الشبكات العصبية) & Deep Learning (التعلم العميق):** The biological inspiration behind modern AI and why depth matters.
* **Generative AI (الذكاء الاصطناعي التوليدي):** How models like ChatGPT are trained and how to use prompt engineering effectively.
* **Responsible AI (الذكاء الاصطناعي المسؤول):** Understanding bias, fairness, and the ethical implications of deploying ML models in the real world.
